The Batman

The Caped Crusadaer

  • Year 1 Bruce returns to Gotham after a long absence. He is 25. He becomes Batman and begins fighting crime. At first, his opponents are ordinary gangsters and mob bosses. As time passes, he begins building his rogues gallery. He encounters Joker, Catwoman, and a few other early foes.
  • Year 2 Batman's hopes of building a better Gotham seem dashed when Harvey Dent becomes Two Face. His cast of villains also continues to grow
  • Year 3 Batman trains the 12 year old Dick Grayson to be his sidekick. Their early adventures are a little more light-hearted. They encounter Riddler, Mad Hatter, and a giant robotic dinosaur.
  • Year 4 More light-hearted adventures. Batman and Robin encounter foes like Killer Moth, Firefly, and Mr. Freeze. In addition, the Bat-Family grows a little. This is the period in which we meet Ace the Bat-hound, the original Batwoman, and Bat-Mite.
  • Year 5 The 30 year old Batman joins the Justice League. The original Bat-Girl joins the Bat-Family.
  • Year 6 The Bat-Family splits up. Ace the Bat-hound is killed. Batwoman and Bat-Girl retire. Bat-Mite stops spending time on Earth. The 15 year old Robin starts spending time with the newly formed Teen Titans. 16 year old Barbara Gordon becomes the new Batgirl. Batman encounters the League of Assassins. Unbeknownst to him, he impregnates Talia al Ghul.
  • Year 7 Batman's younger cousin, the 18 year old Helena Wayne, discovers his identity. He trains her to fight crime as Huntress.
  • Year 8 The 17 year old Robin joins the New Teen Titans.
  • Year 9 Batman trains the 13 year old Jason Todd to be the new Robin. Dick Grayson becomes Nightwing.
  • Year 10 Jason Todd dies. The 20 year old Barbara Gordon is crippled.
  • Year 11 The 13 year old Tim Drake becomes the new Robin. Barbara Gordon becomes Oracle.
  • Year 12 The 36 year old Batman has his back broken by Bane. A new Batman briefly begins patrolling Gotham. However, he's too violent. Batman undergoes physical therapy and then fights the new Batman.
  • Year 13 The 15 year old Robin joins Young Justice. Gotham is devastated by an Earthquake. Cassandra Cain becomes Batgirl. At age 23, Oracle starts the Birds of Prey.
  • Year 14 Lex Luthor becomes president. He declares Batman a criminal. However, he is eventually forced to resign. Later, the 18 year old Jason Todd is revealed to be alive. He fights Batman as the Red Hood.
  • Year 15 The 39 year old Batman meets his son, the 9 year old Damian Wayne. He has a mind bending fight against Doctor Hurt, and then dies killing Darkseid.
  • Year 16 The 25 year old Dick Grayson briefly becomes Batman with Damian Wayne becoming Robin. After a time, Batman returns and starts Batman Inc. And so, we arrive in the present. Batman is 40. Huntress is 27. Barbara Gordon is 26. Nightwing is 25. Red Hood is 20. Red Robin is 18.
